--- layout: default title: Basic Epub Reader for Ubuntu ---

Beru lets you read DRM-free Epub books on your Ubuntu desktop or touch device. It tries to be unobtrusive, letting you manage your books as you like. Your place in your books is saved to Ubuntu One, so you can pick up reading on any of your devices.

Beru is availble in the Ubuntu Software Store, as click package, and in a PPA. It is developed on GitHub and licensed under the GPLv3.
